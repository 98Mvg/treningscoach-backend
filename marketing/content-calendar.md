### Norwegian
```
#trening #aicoach #pulscoaching #smarttrening #treningscoach #coachi #treningsapp #fitnessnorge #styrketrening #lopning
```

### English
```
#AIcoach #pulsecoaching #smartfitness #workouttech #coachi #fitnessAI #fittech #wearabletech #personaltrainer #workoutapp
```

### Usage
- Instagram: Max 20 hashtags per post. Mix of niche (#pulscoaching) and broad (#trening).
- TikTok: Max 5 hashtags. Focus on trending + niche.
- Always include `#coachi` on every post.

## Content Types Per Platform

### Instagram
- **Grid posts** (1:1): Polished branded images with short captions
- **Stories** (9:16): Polls, behind-the-scenes, quick demos
- **Reels**: Short workout clips with Coachi voice overlay (future)

### TikTok
- **Cover images** (9:16): Eye-catching thumbnails for video content
- **Video concepts**: "What if your coach felt your pulse?", reaction videos, first-time user experience
- **Duets/Stitches**: React to fitness content with Coachi angle (future)
